title: "Legacy Deprecation Archive now open"
slug: 2026-01-archive-opens
date: 2026-01-15
category: Program milestone
---

The National Designation Renewal Secretariat is pleased to announce the opening of the **Legacy Deprecation Archive**, established further to Recommendation 1 of the *Legacy Risk Register: National Heritage Exposure Audit* (Meridian Narrative Partners, 2025).

The Archive will provide a complete, accurate, and permanent record of every heritage asset scheduled for sunset, ensuring that no element of the national past is deprecated without full documentation of its origin, development, significance, and the people connected to it. Quarterly review cycles will ensure each record is revisited and re-read indefinitely.

The first entry, [Asset File 0001: "Canada"](/archive/canada), is now available. Canadians are encouraged to consult the Archive regularly to stay informed about what is scheduled to be lost.
