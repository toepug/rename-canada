The Secretariat wishes to advise Canadians that the hyphenation of "forward-compatible" in section 4.6 of the *Brand Transition Feasibility Study (Phase 0 Interim)* has been standardized. No other element of the 214-page document has changed.

The Secretariat thanks the member of the public who reported the inconsistency, whose submission was processed in 15 months, within the standard window.
