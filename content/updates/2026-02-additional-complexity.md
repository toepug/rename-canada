The Secretariat today confirms that Phase 0 scoping activities have identified additional complexity.

The finding follows a nine-week internal review of the previous scoping cycle, which had itself been initiated to assess complexity identified in the cycle before it. The Secretariat considers the consistency of these results to be an encouraging indicator of methodological soundness.

Work continues on schedule. The schedule has been referred to Phase 0 scoping.
