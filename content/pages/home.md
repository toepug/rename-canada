# Canada Name Transition Pilot Program

**From:** National Designation Renewal Secretariat

The Government's designation renewal agenda is advancing through Phase 0 of the Canada Name Transition Pilot Program, a whole-of-nation initiative to identify, evaluate, and ultimately transition to a national identifier that reflects who we are becoming, unburdened by reference to who we have been.

The Secretariat is committed to clear communication. In plain language: the program is proceeding, the phase is Phase 0, and no change of any kind has occurred or is scheduled to occur at this time. We thank Canadians for their patience, which projections indicate will continue to be required.
