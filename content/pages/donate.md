# Support the Program

**From:** National Designation Renewal Secretariat

> ⚠ **DONATION INTAKE — PHASE 0 SCOPING.** Projected acceptance of contributions: 2029.

The Secretariat has received expressions of interest from Canadians wishing to financially support the Canada Name Transition Pilot Program. This support is acknowledged and appreciated.

Donation intake mechanisms, including payment processing, receipting, and a determination of the Program's charitable status, are under active consideration as part of Phase 0 scoping. The Secretariat is unable to accept contributions at this time and cannot provide an earlier estimate.
