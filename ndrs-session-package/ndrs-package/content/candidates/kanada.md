# Candidate Designation 2024-CN-002: Kanada

**Status:** Active — Shortlisted (Phase 0)
**Secretariat assessment:** Preferred Candidate ★
**Stakeholder consultation:** COMPLETE ✓

Kanada was developed by Meridian Narrative Partners as the principal deliverable of a focused four-week Orthographic Modernization Sprint, conducted as an addendum to the firm's original discovery engagement. Total development investment: $310,000 (excl. HST).

Meridian's Orthographic Refresh Addendum (14 pages, 11 of which describe methodology) characterizes Kanada as "a bold reimagining that honours legacy while signalling transformation." The Addendum notes that the designation "achieves near-total continuity of pronunciation, spelling, and public recognition while delivering the full strategic value of a national renaming."

## Compliance assessment

Under the Program's core criterion, a successor designation must carry no association with any historical event whatsoever. The Secretariat acknowledges that questions have been raised regarding the resemblance of Kanada to the current national designation.

Following internal review, the Secretariat has determined that the substitution of the initial letter constitutes sufficient decoupling from historical precedent. This determination is final.

The Secretariat further notes that Candidate Designation 2024-CN-002 is unrelated to the rejected designation *Kanata* (see Rejected Designations Annex, ref. RD-001, rejected: excessively rooted in heritage). The two designations differ by one letter, which under section 4.2 of the Program's Designation Assessment Framework constitutes full semantic separation.
