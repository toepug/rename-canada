# Candidate Designation 2024-CN-004: Maplea

**Status:** Active — Shortlisted (Phase 0)
**Stakeholder consultation:** COMPLETE ✓

Maplea was developed by Meridian Narrative Partners and evaluated through four rounds of national focus group testing. Across all sessions, participants found the designation inoffensive. Maplea recorded the lowest negative-sentiment score of any candidate tested (2.1%), and no participant reported strong feelings of any kind.

Meridian's Focus Group Synthesis Report identifies this profile as "best in class for friction-free adoption," noting that the designation "asks nothing of the public and offers the same in return."

## Compliance assessment

The Secretariat acknowledges that questions have been raised regarding the resemblance of the designation's leading element to the maple tree (*Acer saccharum*) and its associated symbolism. Following internal review, the Secretariat has determined that the terminal vowel sufficiently dissociates the designation from the tree, its leaf, and any related emblems. This determination is consistent with the interpretive precedent established under Candidate Designation 2024-CN-002.
