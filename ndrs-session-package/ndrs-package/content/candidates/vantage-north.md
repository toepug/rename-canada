# Candidate Designation 2024-CN-003: Vantage North

**Status:** Active — Shortlisted (Phase 0)
**Stakeholder consultation:** COMPLETE ✓

Vantage North was developed by Meridian Narrative Partners through a quantitative naming study conducted across six census metropolitan areas. The designation performs strongly with the 25–54 demographic, recording a net sentiment lift of +14 points against baseline and top-quartile scores for "modernity," "confidence," and "approachability."

Performance among respondents aged 55 and over was assessed as within acceptable parameters. Performance among respondents aged 18–24 was not measured, as the segment was reallocated to a future research phase during budget reconciliation.

## Compliance assessment

The designation comprises two elements. "Vantage" was confirmed to carry no association with any historical event, figure, or tradition. With respect to "North," the Secretariat has determined that cardinal directions predate recorded history and are therefore not historical. This determination has been added to the Designation Assessment Framework as interpretive guidance (section 4.7).
