Canada presented by [SPONSOR PENDING] was developed internally by the Secretariat's Commercial Partnerships Office in recognition of the significant naming-rights value embodied in a G7 nation. Preliminary modelling indicates that presenting-partner revenue could offset up to 0.4% of total program transition costs.

Negotiations have been ongoing since the third quarter of 2023 and continue to progress within Phase 0 parameters.

## Compliance assessment

The Secretariat acknowledges that the designation retains the current national identifier as its leading element. This retention is a contractual requirement of the prospective partner category and has been assessed as transitional.

With respect to the presenting partner, the Secretariat has determined that commercial heritage constitutes goodwill rather than history, and the designation is therefore compliant with the Program's core criterion.
